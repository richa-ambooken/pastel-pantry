Chicken Rice

Category: Main Course

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak rice for 20 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and bay leaf.
  3. Add onions and sauté till golden brown. Then add ginger garlic paste.
  4. Now add tomatoes and cook till soft. Add turmeric, chili powder, and garam masala.
  5. Add chicken pieces and sauté till chicken turns white and starts to cook.
  6. Add rice and mix gently with the chicken and masala.
  7. Pour in 3 cups of water. Add salt. Cover and cook till rice is done.
  8. Fluff gently, garnish with coriander, and serve warm with raita or pickle.
