Burnt Basque Cheesecake

Category: Dessert

Ingredients:

  • 200g cream cheese (softened)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (430°F). Line a 6-inch round cake tin with parchment paper, allowing it to overhang.
  2. Beat cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar and beat until combined.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each.
  4. Add cream, flour, vanilla, and salt. Mix until silky smooth.
  5. Pour into lined tin. Tap to release bubbles.
  6. Bake for 25–30 minutes until top is deeply golden brown and jiggly in the center.
  7. Cool at room temp, then refrigerate for 4–6 hours or overnight.
  8. Peel back parchment and slice gently. Serve chilled or at room temp.
