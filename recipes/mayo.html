Homemade Mayonnaise

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg (room temperature)
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar or lemon juice
  • 1 cup neutral oil (like sunflower or canola)
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: ½ tsp sugar for sweetness

Instructions

  1. In a tall jar or bowl, add egg, mustard, vinegar, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Pour the oil over the mixture without stirring.
  3. Insert an immersion blender all the way to the bottom and turn it on.
  4. Hold it steady at the bottom for 5–8 seconds as the mayo emulsifies.
  5. Slowly move the blender upwards to mix the top layer.
  6. Blend until thick, creamy, and pale white in color.
  7. Taste and adjust salt, acidity, or sweetness.
  8. Refrigerate and use within a week.
