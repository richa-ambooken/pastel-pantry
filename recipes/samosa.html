Beef Samosa

Category: Snacks

Ingredients:

  • 250g minced beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Oil for frying
  • Samosa wrappers (or homemade dough)

Instructions:

  1. Heat a little oil in a pan. Add onions, chilies, and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until soft.
  2. Add minced beef and cook until browned.
  3. Add turmeric, cumin, garam masala, and salt. Cook until beef is fully cooked and dry.
  4. Mix in chopped coriander leaves and let it cool.
  5. Fill samosa wrappers with beef filling. Fold into triangle shapes and seal the edges.
  6. Heat oil in a deep pan. Fry samosas until golden and crisp.
  7. Drain on paper towels. Serve hot with chutney.
