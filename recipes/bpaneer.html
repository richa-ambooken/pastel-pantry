Paneer Butter Masala

Category: Main Course

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 2 large tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander to garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil and butter in a pan. Add chopped onion and sauté until golden.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste and cook until aromatic.
  3. Pour in tomato puree and cook until oil begins to separate.
  4. Add turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, sugar, and salt.
  5. Stir well and simmer for 5 mins. Add cream and mix gently.
  6. Add paneer cubes and crushed kasuri methi. Let it simmer for another 3–4 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with naan or rice.
