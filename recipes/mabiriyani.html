Malabar Chicken Biryani

Category: Biriyani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g Chicken (bone-in pieces)
  • 2 large onions (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 3 green chilies (slit)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Whole spices: bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom
  • 4 tbsp ghee
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water for cooking rice

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 mins. Cook it with whole spices and salt until 90% done. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil and ghee. Fry sliced onions till golden brown. Remove half for garnish.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, sauté. Add tomatoes, spices, and cook till mushy.
  4. Add chicken, salt, and yogurt. Cook covered until chicken is tender and masala is thick.
  5. Add chopped mint and coriander. Mix well.
  6. Layer rice over the chicken masala. Top with reserved fried onions and a few drops of ghee.
  7. Cover and cook on low (dum) for 10–15 mins. Let it rest for 10 mins before serving.
