Beef Burger

Category: Snacks

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion rings
  • Ketchup, mustard, or mayo
  • Butter for toasting buns

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix ground beef, onion, egg, breadcrumbs, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Shape into 4 equal-sized patties.
  3. Heat a pan or grill and cook patties on both sides until browned and cooked through.
  4. Toast buns lightly with a bit of butter on a pan.
  5. Assemble the burger: bottom bun, lettuce, beef patty, cheese, tomato, onion rings, sauces, and top bun.
  6. Serve warm with fries or a side salad!
