Butter Chicken

Category: Main Course

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into cubes)
  • 3 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter + 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek)
  • Fresh coriander leaves to garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, lemon juice, chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Let it rest for 30 mins.
  2. Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add onions and sauté until golden.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and cook until raw smell goes.
  4. Stir in tomato puree and cook till oil separates.
  5. Add marinated chicken. Cook on medium flame until chicken is fully cooked.
  6. Sprinkle sugar, kasuri methi, and pour in cream. Mix gently.
  7. Simmer for 5 more minutes. Garnish with coriander.
