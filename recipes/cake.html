Strawberry Shortcake

Category: Dessert

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 2 tbsp sugar (for strawberries)
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tbsp powdered sugar (for cream)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla (for cream)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Cut in butter until crumbly.
  3. Add milk and vanilla; mix until dough forms. Gently knead and pat into a 1-inch thick circle.
  4. Cut into rounds and place on baking tray. Bake for 12-15 minutes until golden. Let cool.
  5. Mix strawberries with 2 tbsp sugar. Let sit to release juices.
  6. Whip heavy cream with powdered sugar and vanilla until soft peaks form.
  7. Slice cooled biscuits in half. Layer with whipped cream and strawberries. Top with the other half.
  8. Serve immediately or chill slightly before serving.
