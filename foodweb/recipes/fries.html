Loaded Fries

Category: Snacks

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes (cut into fries)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 1/4 cup cooked bacon bits (optional)
  • 2 tbsp chopped green onions
  • 2 tbsp sour cream or mayonnaise
  • 1/2 tsp paprika or chili flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Toss potato fries in olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread evenly on the tray.
  3. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until crispy, flipping halfway through.
  4. Remove from oven and sprinkle cheese while fries are hot.
  5. Top with bacon bits, green onions, paprika or chili flakes.
  6. Drizzle with sour cream or mayo. Serve immediately!
