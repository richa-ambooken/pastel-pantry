Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter (plus more for cooking)
  • Optional: 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat. Lightly butter it.
  4. Pour ¼ cup batter for each pancake onto the pan.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and edges look set (about 2–3 minutes).
  6. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown (1–2 minutes).
  7. Serve warm with butter, maple syrup, berries, or chocolate drizzle!
