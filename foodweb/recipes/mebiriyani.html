Meghana Biriyani

Category: Biriyani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (preferably boneless)
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp biriyani masala
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom)
  • 4 tbsp oil + 2 tbsp ghee
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the rice with salt and whole spices until 90% done. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil and ghee, fry onions until golden brown. Reserve a few for garnish.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, biriyani masala, and cook until tomatoes are soft.
  5. Mix in chicken and salt. Cook until chicken is tender and coated with the masala.
  6. Add yogurt, mint, and coriander. Let it simmer and thicken.
  7. Layer cooked rice over the chicken. Top with fried onions and a bit of ghee.
  8. Cover and cook on dum (very low heat) for 15 minutes. Let it rest before serving.
