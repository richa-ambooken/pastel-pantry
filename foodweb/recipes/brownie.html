Brownie

Category: Dessert

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Melt butter and mix with sugar and eggs.
  3. Add dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour into tray and bake for 25 mins.
