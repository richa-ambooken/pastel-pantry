Homemade Pizza

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 ¼ tsp (1 packet) instant yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, olives, etc.)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine flour, salt, sugar, and yeast.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil. Mix and knead for 8–10 minutes until smooth.
  3. Cover and let the dough rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  5. Roll out the dough into a circle and place it on a greased baking tray or pizza stone.
  6. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  7. Sprinkle mozzarella and your favorite toppings.
  8. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  9. Slice and serve hot!
